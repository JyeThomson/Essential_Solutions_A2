Project Idea



This is a project proposal by Essential_Solutions to design a music composition software tool. This software will draw on a bank of music theory knowledge to intelligently suggest ideas to the user in real time during writing/composition, as well as automate tasks which are unnecessarily repeated in this process. In addition, we aim to make this tool accessible to a wider market than just professional creatives - the options available to the user will be labelled with familiar keywords such as ‘emotion’, ‘temperature’, ‘complexity’, rather than technical music jargon. While the technical requirements for this project are ostensibly achievable, the potential outcome could be bigger than the sum of the parts - enhancing our natural creative ability with the power of cutting edge processing technology.
