Team Profile


The Essential_Solutions team is made up of members with varying but compatible and useful personality types. We have auditory, visual, and tactile learners. These different learning styles will provide differing perspectives on the same information which will assist us when tackling tricky questions. We have leaders, ideas people and people with cohesive personality types that will assist in keeping the team tightly knit, cooperative and productive.
